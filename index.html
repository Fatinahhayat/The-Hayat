Our Mission

The Hayat aims to source products made by local artisans all over the world, bringing you quality, handmade, beautiful and unique products whilst working with each community to give back.


We want to take you on an exciting journey with us, not only showing you who has made your product but also how your product has been made and how your purchase is helping the communities we work with.
