It All Started...

When Founder,

Fatinah Hayat, made a decision take a career break from the fashion industry and headed off to Bali. Whilst in Bali, she discovered a deep love of learning about crafts such as batik and jewellery making. She also found enjoyment in spending time with people and learning about their lives, traditions and cultures. From and love of people and handmade products, The Hayat was born.


Hayat, in Arabic means life. The Hayat is all about bringing people together and celebrating our differences. We offer our customers beautiful and unique products which have a story to tell.


Our aim is to work directly with artisans, showcasing how the products are made, who has made the products and to work with each community to learn whilst giving back.
